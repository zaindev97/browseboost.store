Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 3, 2026 · Browse Boost

1. Who We Are

Browse Boost is a software company that develops Chrome browser extensions. This policy covers all products published by Browse Boost, including ProductVid.

2. Information We Collect

  • Email address — when you create an account or use the guest download feature
  • Subscription status — to manage your plan (free or Pro)
  • Download count — to enforce monthly limits on free accounts

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your account and manage your subscription
  • To enforce usage limits on free accounts
  • To send transactional emails (billing receipts from Lemon Squeezy)
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing

4. Video Processing (ProductVid)

When using Pro mode, video stream URLs are temporarily processed on our servers. Output files are automatically deleted within 24 hours. We never permanently store video content.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — Authentication and database (supabase.com)
  • Lemon Squeezy — Payment processing (lemonsqueezy.com)

6. Data Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Passwords are never stored in plain text. We use JWT-based authentication.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at support@browseboost.io.
