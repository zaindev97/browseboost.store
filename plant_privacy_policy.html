Privacy Policy

App: Plant Care Reminder · Last updated: 20 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Plant Care Reminder app ("the app", "we") handles your information. By using the app you agree to this policy.

1. Information stored on your device

The app is designed to work offline. The plants you add, their schedules, your care history, notes, and any growth-journal photos you take are stored only on your device. We do not have an account system and we do not upload this content to any server.

2. Information collected by third parties (ads)

The free version of the app shows ads using Google AdMob. To serve ads, Google and its partners may collect and process certain information, such as your device's advertising identifier, general device information, and approximate (non-precise) location derived from your IP address. This is used to show and measure ads.

You can learn more, and control ad personalization, here: Google Ads & Privacy. You can reset or limit your advertising ID in your device settings (Settings → Privacy → Ads).

Upgrading to the paid "Pro" version removes all ads.

3. Purchases

Pro subscriptions are processed by Google Play Billing. We do not receive or store your payment details; these are handled by Google. See the Google Privacy Policy.

4. Permissions we use

  • Notifications — to send you watering and fertilizing reminders.
  • Camera / Photos — only if you choose to add a growth-journal photo. Photos stay on your device.
  • Internet — to load ads (free version) and process purchases.

5. Data sharing & selling

We do not sell your personal information. The only data shared with third parties is the advertising data described in Section 2, collected by Google AdMob.

6. Children

The app is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Deleting your data

Because your data lives on your device, you can remove it at any time by deleting plants in the app or uninstalling the app.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
