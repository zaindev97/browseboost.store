Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Finds & Favorites (“we,” “us,” or “our”), operating browseboost.store and related social media channels, handles information. We are a product-recommendation publisher: we create review and roundup content and share links to products available on third-party retailers such as Amazon. We are committed to keeping data collection to an absolute minimum.

Information We Collect

  • We do not require accounts and do not knowingly collect personal information such as your name, address, or payment details on this website.
  • Basic analytics: like most websites, our host may automatically log non-identifying technical data (e.g., browser type, approximate region, pages viewed) to help us understand traffic and improve content.
  • Cookies: third-party services (see below) may set cookies to measure referrals and clicks. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

Our Automation Tool & the Pinterest API

We operate an internal tool that publishes content (“Pins”) to our own Pinterest business account through Pinterest’s official API. This tool accesses only our own account to create and manage our own posts. It does not collect, store, access, or process the personal data of any other Pinterest users.

Third-Party Services

  • Amazon Associates: Links to products are affiliate links. When you click them and make a purchase, Amazon may set cookies and we may earn a commission. Your interactions on Amazon are governed by Amazon’s own privacy policy.
  • Pinterest, YouTube & other platforms: Where we publish content, those platforms collect and process data under their own privacy policies, not ours.

Affiliate Disclosure

As an Amazon Associate, we earn from qualifying purchases. Some links on our website and social channels are affiliate links, meaning we may receive a small commission at no extra cost to you. This never affects the price you pay.

How We Use Information

  • To understand which content is helpful and improve what we publish.
  • To measure referral clicks so affiliate programs can attribute purchases correctly.

We do not sell your personal information.

Your Choices

  • You can browse our website without providing personal information.
  • You can disable or clear cookies in your browser at any time.
  • You can contact us to ask any question about this policy (see below).

Children’s Privacy

Our content is intended for a general adult audience and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
